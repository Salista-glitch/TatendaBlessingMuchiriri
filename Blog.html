Latest Episode

Empowering Communities Through Montessori on Wheels.

Join Tatenda Blessing Muchiriri as he shares the inspiring journey of Rungano Montessori on Wheels. In this episode, Tatenda discusses how his mobile classroom brings education directly to Black and Brown communities, ensuring that children and families can learn, play, and dream together. Through hands-on learning, parent partnerships, and small group experiences, he is making Montessori education more accessible and empowering families to take an active role in their children's learning.

Latest Episode

Tatenda Muchiriri: A Global Montessori Advocate.

In this episode of The Montessori Mission podcast, host Charlotte Awdry speaks with Tatenda Blessing Muchiriri, a dedicated Montessori educator and advocate. From discovering Montessori in Beijing to earning certifications and leading initiatives in Colorado, Tatenda shares his journey of bringing Afro-Indigenous educational practices to underserved communities. As a board member and speaker, he is committed to expanding access to Montessori education and social justice-driven learning. Tune in to hear how his innovative classroom-on-wheels is transforming education for families in need.

Latest Episode

Bringing Montessori Education to Every Community.

In this recorded live Q&A, Tatenda Muchiriri, founder of Montessori on Wheels, shares how his global journey—from Zimbabwe to China, Kenya, and Colorado—has shaped his mission to make Montessori education more inclusive and accessible. He discusses the power of culturally responsive learning, the challenges of addressing stereotypes in education, and practical ways to foster diversity in classrooms. Discover how Montessori on Wheels is breaking barriers and inspiring a new generation of globally aware learners.
